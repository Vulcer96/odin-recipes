Madras

Description

Beautiful chicken curry with a tomato base and a slight spice level

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Madras curry powder
  • Chopped tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Bell pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop onions, ginger and garlic and fry in butter until soft. Add spices and fry for a further 2 minutes
  2. Brown chicken then add chopped tomatos. Let the spices infuse and squash tomatos.
  3. Add bell peppers and simmer for 45 minutes, serve with rice
