Cheese pasta

Description

Creamy cheese pasta with chicken and celery

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Chicken Stock
  • Onion
  • Oregano
  • Celery
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in stock with onion and celery add salt,pepper,oregano for seasoning
  2. Add cheese of choice for sauce and slowly stir to melt
  3. cook the pasta and add to the chicken, save a cup of pasta water to add for consistency
