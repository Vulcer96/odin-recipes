Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Beef mince in tomato sauce with carrots and celery on a bed of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Beef mince
  • Red wine
  • Chicken Stock
  • Tomato pureé
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Rosemary
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add the beef,onion, carrot and celery to a pan and fry off all the juices
  2. Add red wine, once the alcohol is fried off and chicken stock, tomato pureé, rosemary, salt, pepper and milk and simmer for an hour and a half
  3. cook the pasta and server when ready. Garnish with basil and season to taste
